xAI

Grok — chat and API models from xAI, integrated with X (Twitter) for subscribers in many regions. Known for “spicy” branding and rapid iteration; Grok-2 open-weight releases made headlines. See xAI (company) and Grok (chatbot), March 2026 snapshot.

← AI Model Museum

Architecture & training (overview)

Public technical detail is sparse vs OpenAI/Google. Grok models are large-scale Transformer stacks; Grok-1 weights were open-sourced (Apache 2.0) in 2024 as a 314B-parameter MoE (per announcement). Later generations emphasize real-time X data access and multimodal features in product.

Official: x.ai

Tiers & naming

  • Grok-1 — first open-weights milestone (MoE).
  • Grok-1.5 / 2 / 3 / 4 — sequential generations; higher numbers = frontier capability in xAI messaging.
  • Modes — Normal vs Fun / persona toggles in consumer UI (not separate models).
  • Heavy — some menus expose a higher-compute / reasoning path (product naming varies).

Launch & benchmarks (selected)

Grok-1 (2023) launched as a ChatGPT competitor for X Premium+. Grok-2 (2024) added image generation and stronger benchmarks; portions released as open weights. Grok-3 (early 2025) claimed SOTA narratives in public demos. Grok-4 (2025–2026) appears in frontier IDE pickers alongside GPT-5.x and Claude 4.x.
