Anthropic

Claude — assistant and API models built with Constitutional AI and safety research narratives. Tiers Opus (depth), Sonnet (balanced), Haiku (speed) persist across generations. See Anthropic and Claude (language model), March 2026 snapshot.

← AI Model Museum

Architecture & training (overview)

Claude models are decoder-only transformers with long-context design (100K+ token class in Claude 2 era; later 200K+ product claims). Training emphasizes RLHF-style alignment and Anthropic’s constitutional preference modeling — exact data mixes and parameter counts are largely undisclosed for frontier models.

Official: anthropic.com · Claude API docs.

Tiers & naming

  • Opus — maximum capability / coding depth; flagship for agents.
  • Sonnet — daily-driver balance; Thinking variants in IDEs add reasoning budget.
  • Haiku — fastest / lowest cost; summaries and high-volume tasks.
  • Generations — Claude 3, 3.5, 4, 4.5 denote major capability jumps in product naming.

Launch & benchmarks (selected)

Claude 3 (March 2024) introduced Opus / Sonnet / Haiku with strong multimodal benchmarks. Claude 3.5 Sonnet (mid-2024) often matched or exceeded prior Opus-class tasks at lower cost. Claude 4 and 4.5 (2025–2026) continue the Opus/Sonnet/Haiku structure with extended reasoning and coding focus.
